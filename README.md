# Dont-Block-Me 
## "Something is connected that should not be."
<img width="747" height="611" alt="Screenshot 2026-04-29 093754" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/c671cf34-130d-44b1-9832-78f3bd670700" />

A network-security horror game built with Godot.
You play as a system admin trying to purge an anomalous "Listener" from your subnet.

## The Objective
Identify: Look through the list of connected devices
Analyze: Real devices have low Packet Loss (PL). The anomaly has high latency and high PL.
Terminate: Click on the suspicious node before it indexes your entire home network.
Survive: Reach Round 50 to disconnect the Listener permanently.


## Features
Real-time Audio Engine: A dynamic "Heartbeat" and "Fan" system that reacts to the game's 
horror level.
Visual Distortion: Chromatic aberration, scanlines, and glitch effects that intensify as the 
network becomes compromised.
Adaptive AI: The anomaly will begin to spoof device names and distort the interface as you 
progress.
BIOS Simulation: Custom game-over state modeled after legacy American Megatrends BIOS screens.

#### WARNING: If you hear breathing on localhost, do not look behind you. Keep your eyes on the terminal.
